CCTV Systems

Zahra Security has end to end solutions with the following products for CCTV.

Pelco

Pelco specialises in Closed Circuit Television Systems - CCTV. Pelco’s wide range of CCTV products address customers’ various application requirements. Pelco products are manufactured in accordance to the highest industry standards. Zahra’s business relationship with Pelco as a partner and distributor for over a decade has spread to various countries in the Middle East and Africa.

Axis

Axis provides IP-based network video solutions including network cameras (IP cameras) and video encoders for security surveillance and remote monitoring.

Sony

Sony has been the master of CCD technology and is the leading provider of latest CCDs to all the major manufacturer across the world. Zahra has partnered up with Sony to launch their new advanced 4K and other solution in the market.

Riva

The name RIVA stands for Realtime Intelligent Video Analytics, an intelligent 3D – video analytic system, which is already installed in all devices as a standard feature at no extra costs. Additionally to the already installed basic version, several innovative analytic filters for special applications can be purchased at low costs. Due to an intuitive 3D graphic interface, the video analytics can be set up quickly and are very easy to operate. Partnered up with Harco Group, Zahra has a complete solution for analytics based CCTV solution at very cost effective budgets.

Honeywell

Honeywell has CCTV solutions to fulfill all sorts of market needs. From very high requirement to a very basic solution, Honeywell has all to offer.

Securtex

Video cameras, CCTV monitoring and guards are not enough in today’s high-risk environments. Having the ability to act upon real-time, visual or event-created information is a necessity. A dedicated manufacturer who works separately for each requirement with specialized solutions is the identity of Securtex.

Video Managment Solutions

More than screening your vicinity, it’s the management of recorded data which is of utmost importance. Video management solutions are your head end software and hardware solutions where all your surveilled data is recorded, processed and automatically managed to meet your needs. Zahra has following solutions for VMS:

  1. PELCO ENDURA: Scalable to accept thousands of high-definition IP cameras, the Endura IP VMS is an intelligent, yet easy-to-use approach to video security that provides greater control, enhanced operational flexibility, centralized management, cost-effective recording, and integration capabilities. Endura has been engineered to offer the quality and responsiveness of traditional analog installations with all the benefits of an IP VMS system. What’s more, Endura offers several options for existing analog matrix customers to migrate to an IP VMS system at their own pace.
  2. EXACQ VISION: The exacqVision VMS software records surveillance video from Thousands of IP camera models and displays on a free Windows, Linux or OSX client software, web browser or mobile device. The intuitive exacqVision software can be licensed for small simple installations to demanding enterprise applications using exacqVision Start, exacqVision Professional, exacqVision Enterprise or exacqVision Edge software.

Access Control System

Just like the video solutions, Zahra Security has End to End solutions when it comes to access control systems. Following are the major solutions offered by Zahra Security.

Lenel

Zahra Security partners with Lenel since 1999 and uses Lenel’s technology in designing solutions for Physical and Logical Access management, Integrated ID Management, Network Security, Video Management and Automated Video Analytics.

S2 Security

: Enterprise-class, feature-rich S2 access control systems integrate credential-based access control, intrusion detection and video security applications for a unified management and administration experience. 100% web based, these IP networked systems require no software installation or client license fees. Flexible system architecture allows for a range of applications from single sites to globally distributed physical security management systems. S2 building access control systems also integrate with legacy access control systems, allowing for easy upgrades.

Honeywell

Honeywell Access is a leader in the field of access control, security management and automation systems. Honeywell Access' entry into the Access Control industry began in 1982 with the introduction of the N-1000 control panel.

Our range of products extends from single door access control to integrated systems capable of managing thousands of doors and alarm monitoring points at worldwide locations.

Intercom System

Like its other major solutions, Zahra has been an active solution provider for intercom systems since intercom were initially introduced in UAE. Today, Zahra carries major brands in its intercom solutions like Fermax and Aiphone.

When it comes to major solutions like buildings, malls and hospitality, Zahra Security Systems is an exclusive distributor of Aiphone Intercom Systems.

Aiphone

Aiphone has become the most respected and reliable brand of communication systems in the world. Aiphone Co. Limited in Nagoya, Japan exports to over 60 countries. Aiphone Corporation is the Sales, Marketing and Distribution center for North America, and Aiphone S.A.S. is their sales and Distribution center for the European market.

Aiphone products range from simple do-it-yourself door answering units to sophisticated video entry security systems, complex microprocessor-based commercial systems, and luxury condo/apartment security/communication systems. They are unrivaled in design simplicity, technical excellence and reliability.

With over 30 standard systems, and additional manufacturing by special order, we can create a system for virtually any intercom application.

Fermax

FERMAX is a Spanish family business based in Valencia, Spain.

The company, founded by Mr. Fernando Maestre in 1949, is specialized in designing communication and access control systems, both digital and analogue, enjoying a prominent position among the world leading brands.

FERMAX currently has subsidiaries and representative offices in Spain, UK, France, China, India, Dubai, Singapore, Polska, Russia, Colombia, Vietnam, Bahrain, USA and Turkey. Our products are also sold in another 70 countries. Presently, Fermax is synonymous with innovation, technology and design.

Throughout its existence, Fermax has established new standards in these categories and enlarged the market with new inventions.Overall, among utility models and invention patents, FERMAX has registered over 30 inventions with the Spanish Patent and Trademark Office (OEPM). Notably, throughout its history 15 invention patents have been registered with the OEPM, and 25 industrial designs and 17 trademarks at the European Union level.It all began when FERMAX was founded. Fernando Maestre’s first invention was a peculiar device, the 1949 Radio-Intercom.
